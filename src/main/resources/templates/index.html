Who we are!

We are a Non-profit that is changing lives in Africa. We teach coding skills for FREE and place our students with great tech companies. Join us!

The inspiration for this venture came from me attending the Ironyard bootcamp, It has practically changed my outlook on my future and has given me hope that I will remain relevant in this age of technology. I want to share this hope with my students, for instance the youth unemployment rate in Nigeria is over 50%. If through Code Africa we can equip youths with skills to gain employment, we would call this a little win.
